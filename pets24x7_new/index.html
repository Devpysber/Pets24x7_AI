Questions Pet Business Owners Ask Most

Is there a setup fee or monthly cost?
Nope. Listing is 100% free. You only pay for the optional Featured Listing upgrade — and only if you want top-of-page visibility. No commissions. No subscriptions.
How do you display Google ratings?
We link your listing to your existing Google Business profile (via your Google CID). Your live rating, review count and recent customer scores show up on your Pets24x7 listing — and refresh automatically.
Can I list multiple locations or branches?
Yes. Each branch gets its own SEO-optimised listing page, with its own Google reviews and WhatsApp routing. We offer bulk-listing discounts for chains.
What categories of pet businesses can list?
Veterinary clinics, emergency animal hospitals, groomers, boarders, daycare, dog walkers, trainers, sitters, pet taxi & transport, relocators, dental care, physiotherapy, mobile vets, vaccination centres, exotic/avian vets — and more. If you serve pets, we'll find you a category.
How long does it take to receive my first enquiry?
Most businesses get their first WhatsApp enquiry within 5-7 days of going live. Featured listings often see traction in 24-48 hours.

You do. 100%. Pets24x7 never hides customer contact details. Build your CRM, run your own marketing, own your relationships.
