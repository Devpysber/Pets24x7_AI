FAQ

Common Questions from Pet Business Owners

How is Pets24x7 different from a regular SEO agency?
Pets24x7 is purpose-built for pet businesses. We rank specifically for pet-intent searches like "vets near me", "dog grooming in [city]", "24x7 emergency animal hospital" — across 570+ cities. We also give your listing a Pets24x7 directory page on day one, so you get a backlink + visible Google ratings before any paid work begins.
Do you work with single-location pet businesses?
Yes. Most of our clients are single-location vets, groomers, boarders and walkers. Our Starter plan is built for them. Multi-location chains get the Chain plan with branch-level dashboards.
How quickly can I see results?
Listings on Pets24x7 go live within 24-48 hours and start receiving WhatsApp enquiries from week 1. Paid ads typically deliver first leads within 3-7 days. SEO is a 60-90 day game — but we report progress every week.
Are there any contracts or lock-in periods?
No long-term contracts. We work on rolling monthly engagements — if we're not delivering value, you walk. 89% of our clients renew quarter-on-quarter.
Do you handle Google Ads spend or do I pay Google directly?
You pay Google directly using your card — we never touch your media budget. We charge a flat management fee + setup, with full transparency on spend, clicks, conversions and CPL.
